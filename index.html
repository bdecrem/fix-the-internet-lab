Mozilla’s Fix-The-Internet Spring Lab

Whether it’s misinformation, surveillance, shutdowns, centralized platforms + AI algorithms or hateful bots, today’s internet has veered from its FULL potential. Mozilla’s Fix-The-Internet Lab is an incubator-styled program to mobilize + fund products + technologies that enables everyone to connect, build a better society + economy.

Do you and your friends have an idea for how to make things better? Apply now for our Spring Lab, or we can keep you up-to-date on all the details for the Summer Startup Studio.

For the Spring Lab, we are looking for ideas in the following areas:

  • Collaboration & society: in these unique times, we are especially interested in ideas that foster better collaboration online, but also neighborhood support networks and supporting small businesses.
  • Distributed web: using the blockchain and peer-to-peer technologies to build the next generation of the web.
  • Messaging & social networking: can we build the next IG or TikTok, but on stronger foundations?
  • Misinformation & content: ideas for apps & services that help us get beyond polarization, filter bubbles and fake news.
  • Surveillance capitalism: whether it’s Big Tech or governments, everyone’s collecting your data. How do we put the user back in control of their data?
  • Artificial Intelligence put to work to benefit consumers and citizens, not just big tech.
